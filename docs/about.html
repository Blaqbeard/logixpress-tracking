About LogiXpress

Revolutionizing logistics through technology, innovation, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, LogiXpress began as a small team of logistics experts and technologists with a shared vision: to transform the way businesses manage their supply chains through innovative technology solutions.

What started as a simple tracking platform has evolved into a comprehensive logistics ecosystem serving businesses of all sizes across multiple continents. Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of digital logistics transformation.
